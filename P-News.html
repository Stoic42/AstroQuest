Planet Express UCS - frame ‚ÄúGood news everyone! Get ready for a space journey in the 30th century!‚Äù 

Hello everyone, here is the iconic Planet Express ship in a UCS version. I love the animated series and also the spaceship design, so it was a pleasure for me to accept this challenge and start the project. 

It was difficult for the curved profile, that is not simple to create with Lego bricks. This is a big model: the ship counts 5112 parts and the total length is almost 1 m. 

For this reason it needs a very strong inner structure, as you can see from one of the pics in the album. It was totally built using Lego Digital Designer. 

For the moment there are no minifigs, but hopefully they could be added in the future. 

Features:

 - turnable turret

 - mechanism to bring down the cargo elevator

 - UCS plate ;)
